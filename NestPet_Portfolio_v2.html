Project Overview

NestPet E-Commerce — Independent Digital Marketing Project

A self-directed, full-funnel digital marketing engagement designed to demonstrate strategic planning, multi-channel execution, and measurable business impact.

NestPet is a B2C e-commerce pet store targeting the US market. The core audience spans 25–65 years old living in urban and suburban areas, with females accounting for 80% of the total customer base. Both segments share characteristics including household income of $65K–$125K, active lifestyles, and above-average digital literacy and spending propensity.

The company operates with a clear annual vision: increase market share by 20% by year-end, with quarterly objectives set to maintain momentum. My engagement covered Q1, where the business required a coordinated, cross-channel approach to drive growth simultaneously across organic, paid, and retention channels.

As the sole digital marketing strategist on this project, I was independently responsible for diagnosing performance gaps, designing the strategy, building the execution plan, deploying all campaigns, and reporting results — with full accountability for outcomes.

SEMrush Domain Overview — NestPet.com · Q1 Jan–Mar 2026

Strategy 02 · Email
Lifecycle Email Marketing Programme
NestPet had $8,000 in monthly revenue but zero email infrastructure — 62% of the list was inactive with no segmentation and no post-purchase loyalty programme. The task was to build a retention engine from the ground up.
Klaviyo Automation Flows — NestPet · Q1 2026
Actions Taken
  • Segmented the customer database into 6 cohorts: New Customers, VIP Repeat, At-Risk (inactive 60+ days), Browse-No-Buy, Seasonal Buyers, and Cart Abandoners
  • Built 8 email sequences — Welcome series (3 emails, 45% open rate), Post-Purchase nurture (2 emails driving 18% repeat orders), and Re-engagement targeting 2,400 inactive subscribers
  • Designed 7 promotional email templates in Canva: Summer Travel, Pet Care, Holiday Gift Guide, Gift Card Promotion, Discounts, Product Bundle Subscription, and Furry Birthday Special
  • Implemented Klaviyo automation: purchase triggers, cart abandonment flows (sent within 2 hours), and browsing behaviour sequences (24-hour follow-up after product view)
  • A/B tested 18 email variations across subject lines, send times, and CTAs — identified Tuesday 10am as the optimal send window (32% vs 18% baseline open rate)

Strategic Insights — Email
Segmentation is the single highest-leverage action in email marketing — reaching the right persona at the right moment in the right tone consistently outperforms sending a higher volume of undifferentiated messages.
A/B testing works at the level of precise, isolated variables. The Tuesday 10am finding (a 14-percentage-point open rate lift) emerged from testing send time in isolation — not from changing the subject line and content simultaneously.
The $200 gap between $4,800 actual and $5,000 target reflected a one-week automation setup delay at the start of Q1. With full-quarter automation in place, Q2 is on track to exceed this target. The learning: automation deployment time must be budgeted into the launch plan.

Strategy 03 · Google Ads
Google Ads Restructure — Paid Search Optimisation
Inherited a legacy campaign spending $4,200/month at 1.8x ROAS with no audience segmentation and a Quality Score averaging 5/10. Rebuilt the entire account structure to maximise efficiency within the same budget.
Actions Taken
  • Audited 240 active keywords and consolidated into 10 high-intent keyword groups with optimised match types — exact and phrase match for high performers
  • Improved Quality Score from 5/10 to 8.2/10 by refining ad copy, landing pages, and expected CTR alignment
  • Created 17 ad variations testing angles, hooks, product benefits (fast shipping, eco-friendly materials), pain points (allergies, picky eaters), and seasonal messaging
  • Built 8 custom audiences: product category interests, cart abandoners, past site visitors, and look-alike audiences based on high-value customers
  • Implemented conversion tracking across 9 product categories and set up ROAS-based bidding for the most profitable segments
  • Deployed advanced tracking via Google Tag Manager and GA4 — event tracking for add-to-cart, checkout, and purchase; funnel analysis for drop-off identification; attribution modelling for channel contribution

Strategic Insights — Google Ads
A well-structured, optimised site raises paid conversion rate directly. When ad copy, landing page messaging, and URL structure share the same keyword signal, Google rewards it with a higher Quality Score — and a lower cost per click.
Bidding strategy selection is the single highest-leverage decision in any PPC account. Switching to ROAS-based bidding after accumulating sufficient conversion data was the turning point that unlocked the 4.1x ROAS result.
Tracking micro-conversions — wishlist additions, filter interactions, scroll depth — surfaces signals that sales data alone would never reveal. These micro-KPIs informed mid-campaign adjustments that compounded into the macro results.
